Product Design
IDEO for a Financial Services Client, 2011
Our client thought he hired us to build an iPad app for the lower to middle class customers of his prepaid debit card. But what we quickly realized was that the entire customer journey had to be unpacked, understood, and transformed to deliver on the company’s aspiration to be the Southwest Airlines of banking: simple, trustworthy, and value-driven.
We brought to life several concepts that spanned across this journey. Every single idea had to embody and reinforce those three key attributes to build awareness and subsequent loyalty— from the packaging to the account holder’s homepage, our ideas complemented each other, but were also impactful alone.
Additionally, we had to design around elements over which we had little control. Through extensive channel visits, user research, and iteration, we ensured our concepts made sense within the broader retail and financial context. For instance, we created bold packaging to draw attention on a crowded rack. We also investigated mechanisms and applications that went beyond supporting basic financial transactions to ones that addressed the financial aspirations of the customers.
